Mga impormasyon ng Loyalista 4B

First of all, I don’t really know how to start this, but there’s one thing I can truly say our section is the best. It’s honestly my favorite of all. We started out not really knowing each other, and everything was so quiet at first. I even thought we wouldn’t become this close. But as time went by, our section turned out to be full of different talents some are great at drawing, others at acting, some are skilled in editing, and most of all, we are truly competitive. We never back down from any challenge. It’s like our section has everything. Even so, I know that we always do our best with the help of our beloved Angel. She has always been there for us and has taught us so much. We are the chaotic, loud, beautiful, and handsome group in Loyalista and most importantly, we never give up. The 4th quarter has been the happiest and most memorable time for us because we were always laughing and teasing each other. I hope we get to be together again and be classmates once more. -- Montellin, Vera Yvone
